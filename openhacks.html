Hackathon Tracks

1. Interactive Education

Develop games, simulations, or interactive modules that explain complex immunological pathways to middle and school students.

2. Research Visualization

Create dashboards or visualizers that translate raw research data into understandable insights for the broader scientific community.

3. Accessibility Tools

Build assistive technology or platforms that help patients and families navigate the complexities of neuro-inflammatory diagnoses.

4. Outreach Infrastructure

Architect solutions for decentralized science communication, ensuring research outreach reaches unrepresented regions globaly.

48-Hour Sprint

From initial concept to a working prototype, you'll have one weekend to make your mark. Supported by expert mentors in both neuroscience and software development.
