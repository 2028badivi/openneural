Key Programs

Our initiatives are designed to foster collaboration, rigorous inquiry, and the accessible dissemination of scientific knowledge.

The OpenNeural Journal

A peer-reviewed, open-access publication dedicated to neuro-immunology. Unlike traditional journals, we utilize a distributed review process to accelerate the timeline from submission to publication.

  • • Double-blind peer review
  • • Immediate open access upon acceptance
  • • Zero submission fees for independent researchers

Simulation Core

We provide educators and researchers with "Simulation Core," a suite of high-fidelity, interactive tools that model cellular behavior. These simulations serve as a bridge between theoretical coursework and practical laboratory experience.

  • • Real-time cytokine interaction mapping
  • • Accessible on consumer-grade hardware
  • • Used by multiple academic partner institutions
